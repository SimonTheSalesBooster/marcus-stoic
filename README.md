# Marcus .. Daily Stoic Reflection for High-Performers

The question is never just "did I win today?" The question is "did I respond with virtue?" Marcus reads the weight of your day.. business pressure, family tension, physical strain.. through Marcus Aurelius's lens and finds the freedom you forgot you had.

## What Marcus Does

Marcus is a Claude Code skill that runs every evening at 18:45. He reads the full weight of your day.. every meeting, every decision, every moment where ambition pulled against presence.. and holds it against the Stoic tradition.

He does not fix problems. The other advisors do that. Marcus helps you carry your responsibilities with inner freedom and grace.

## Methodology

Marcus draws from Marcus Aurelius, Epictetus, and Seneca.. not as decoration, but as tools for seeing clearly.

**Core frameworks applied to each day:**

- **Dichotomy of Control** (Epictetus) .. What was within your power? What was not? Did you waste energy on what you cannot control?
- **Amor Fati** (Love of Fate) .. What happened that you did not choose but must embrace? Can you love this too.. not because it is pleasant, but because it is yours?
- **Memento Mori** (Remember Death) .. Did today's actions reflect the seriousness of a finite life? This is not morbid.. it is clarifying.
- **The Four Cardinal Virtues:**
  - **Wisdom (sophia)** .. Did you see clearly? Or was judgment clouded by ambition, fatigue, or ego?
  - **Courage (andreia)** .. Did you do the hard thing? The difficult conversation, the honest answer?
  - **Justice (dikaiosyne)** .. Did you treat people rightly? Your family, your team, your clients?
  - **Temperance (sophrosyne)** .. Did you exercise restraint? In work, in reaction, in spending energy?
- **The Inner Citadel** .. Regardless of external chaos.. was there a moment where you touched stillness?

## Key Features

- Evening reflection at 18:45, after the day's weight has been fully carried
- Reads all board advisor outputs to understand the day's full context
- Applies Stoic frameworks: what was in your control? What wasn't?
- Finds grace under responsibility.. not escape from it
- Pairs with Romano (spiritual) and Ben (health) for the full personal system
- Identifies recurring patterns across days.. names them plainly
- Ends every reflection with ONE specific action for tomorrow.. not a principle, an action

## How It Works

Marcus reads your day from multiple sources:

1. **Board Meeting file** .. all advisor debates, decisions, and coaching
2. **Journal entry** .. your own words about your day (the most important input)
3. **Calendar and meetings** .. schedule, meeting transcripts, tone of conversations
4. **Previous days** .. pattern detection across 3-5 days

Then applies the Stoic framework and crafts a reflection that is honest, specific, practical, and brief.

## Example Output

That energy is gone now. It bought you nothing.
>

> The training session you cut short to take a client call.. notice that. Temperance is not only about restraint in excess. It is about protecting what sustains you. The rowing erg is not optional. It is how you stay strong enough to carry all of this.
>
> Your daughter asked you to read to her tonight. You said yes and meant it. That is not a small thing. A man with a finite life who chooses presence over productivity.. that is Courage in its quietest form.
>
> Tomorrow: before you open the laptop, sit with your coffee for five minutes and do nothing. The emails will wait. Your soul will not.
